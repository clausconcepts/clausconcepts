Ownership Structure

What is a bare land condominium?

Coastal Breeze Way is structured as a bare land condominium — a style of ownership common in Nova Scotia that gives you the best of both worlds. You own your individual lot and the home on it outright, while shared infrastructure like roads and common green spaces are maintained collectively by the condo corporation.

Think of it as having the independence of owning your own home, with the convenience of shared upkeep for the things that benefit everyone.

You Own Your Land

Your lot is yours — defined by survey markers. Plant a garden, set up a firepit, make it yours.

Lower Fees

Bare land condo fees are typically a fraction of traditional condo fees since you maintain your own home.

Shared Infrastructure

Roads and common green spaces are collectively maintained by the condo corporation — no surprises, no solo burden.

True Homeownership

Unlike a traditional condo, you're not sharing walls. It's your house, your yard, your space.
