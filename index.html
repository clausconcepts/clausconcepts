Ownership Structure

A bare land condominium — the best of both worlds

Coastal Breeze Way is structured as a bare land condominium under Nova Scotia's Condominium Act. That means you own your lot and your home outright, just like a traditional property — while sharing the cost of common infrastructure like roads, landscaping, and shared green spaces with your neighbours.

It's the independence of owning your own home with the convenience of a well-maintained community — and none of the complexity of a traditional condo.

You Own Your Land

Your lot is yours, defined by survey — build, landscape, and live as you choose.

Lower Fees

Condo fees cover shared infrastructure only — roads, green space, and common areas — keeping costs low.

Shared Maintenance

Snow clearing, road upkeep, and communal landscaping are handled collectively — less for you to worry about.

Community Standards

A well-managed association ensures the neighbourhood stays beautiful and property values are protected.

Your Home, Your Land

All the pride of individual homeownership with the ease of shared community upkeep — a modern approach to residential living in Nova Scotia.
